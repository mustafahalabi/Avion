# Organization — Engineering OS

**Status:** Approved
**Version:** 1.0
**Owner:** CTO
**Approved By:** CEO
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-29

This directory holds the canonical organizational design of Engineering OS — the virtual software company. These documents define how the company is structured: its departments, its positions, who reports to whom, how information and approvals flow, and who is accountable for what. They describe organizational behavior, not product implementation or AI orchestration. Together they are the authoritative source for the company model that the runtime brings to life.
